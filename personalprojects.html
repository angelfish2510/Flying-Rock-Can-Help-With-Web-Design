One Day at a Time - Remote Education Support

Background Information:

When the global pandemic struck teachers of all categories were thrown into the fire. While they did an incredible job piecing things together, the information was very challenging to navigate - especially when combining school and extracurriculars. I threw together this basic site and shared it with fellow parents as well as educators as a way to consolidate the information overload and create some sense of structure. You will find:

  • Categories include 4th grade and Kindergarten education as well as extracurriculars
  • Compliation of resources, links, schedules to assist in the new daily "routine"
  • Additional information was added as it became relevant, removed as it became irrelevant

One Day at a Time - Remote Education Support

While this is a rudimentary site lacking bells and whistles, I include it to demonstrate:
  • Passion for utilizing the skills I had learned at that time
  • Creative application of knowledge for a world full of unknowns
  • Because it was fun
