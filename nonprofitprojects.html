Full Basket Belize

Background Information:

I have been serving on the board for the nonprofit Full Basket Belize as Publicity Chair. One responsibility in this role has been maintaining and updating the website. Prior to my involvement the website was created using Wordpress so much of the structure is not my own. I have included this site because:

  • The organization is incredibly impactful to individuals and communities in Belize
  • I have enjoyed the challenge of working within Wordpress limitations - and it has reiterated that I much prefer building from scratch!
  • I was able to add some great features including temporary pages for specific fundraisers and a history of newsletters
